About ORLED 9

ORLED 9 is a family-owned business established in 2005. We combine long-standing industry experience with a hand-crafted approach to LED installations and event rentals. Our portfolio spans intimate private installations and larger commercial projects, all executed with attention to detail and durability.

Our Services

  • Sales and custom installations of indoor and outdoor LED screens
  • Installation, calibration and maintenance
  • Mobile LED rentals and event technical support
